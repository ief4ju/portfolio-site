Virginia Sports Property Digi Deck

Virginia Sports Properties has moved from Powerpoint to a new online platform called SportsDigita for all of their presentation needs. My role as their designer was to work closely with Sportsdigita's developers and brand manager to create a cohesive user friendly platform for VSP's sales team. During this project I worked on several things including brand alignment, UX/UI design, building an image library, video editing and user testing. Our user testing and feedback have yielded great results and promise a successful tool for VSP's sales team.

Date of Production: January 2017 - August 2017
