Double Exposure

During my last year of college while working with UVA football and studying studio arts, I spent time exploring new design techniques. I was drawn to double exposure because of its unique look and ability to tell a story. My fascination of nature versus man-made inspired me to design graphics displaying UVA football alumni alongside their respective NFL team's skyline. Connecting the energy of these two subjects challenged me and pushed me to design unique and intriguing works of art.

Date of Production: November 2015 - February 2016
