Recruiting Graphics

These are examples of work I created while working for the University of Virginia football office. These images reached thousands of fans across the country and were used for recruitment purposes. I developed the design for these images in collaboration with football coaches and staff in order to provide them with weekly mailers for recruits. This work is featured across social media, email mailers, and print.

Date of Production: March 2014 - June 2016
