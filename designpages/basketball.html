Virginia Basketball

These are examples of work I created while working for the University of Virginia basketball office. These images reached thousands of fans across the country and were used for recruitment purposes. I developed the design for these images in collaboration with basketball coaches and staff in order to provide them with weekly mailers for recruits. This work is featured across social media, email mailers, and print.

Date of Production: January 2016 - May 2016
